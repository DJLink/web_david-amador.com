About me

October 21, 2008 · 1 min · 90 words · David Amador

My name is David Amador and I’m a game developer.
I’ve worked in games for Desktop, Mobile and Console Platforms. I’ve ranged from making my own custom framework engine, which I sometimes talk about here, and also using proprietary engines, like Game Maker and Unity 3D, so I’m a somewhat versatile programmer.

I am the developer of this little game called Quest of Dungeons, among others.
